## About Crème'ai

Crème'ai is an AI-powered DeFi app designed for all retail investors. With Crème'ai you can turn crypto narratives into portfolios within minutes, swap directly across chains and manage your portfolio all in one place. It is self-custodial, fully on-chain, gasless and yield-enabled.

## Why use Crème'ai

Crème’ai offers a simple, streamlined way to curate and manage your crypto portfolios. With Crème’ai, you can:

-   Structure your portfolio clearly with weights using personal indexes, also known as [**Pies**](/using-creme#what-are-personal-indexes)

-   Turn crypto narratives into investable portfolios within minutes

-   Curate, remix, and share portfolio ideas with others

-   Manage and rebalance portfolios across multiple chains seamlessly

-   Access DeFi interactions such as lending and staking through a unified experience

By combining the structure of traditional index products with AI-powered workflows, Creme ai provides users with x.

## Who is Crème'ai for?

It is incredibly difficult to invest effectively in crypto. Emotions kill rational thinking, on-chain complexities dominate decision making, and liquidty constantly chase speculation over quality. Everyone deserves a calmer and smarter way to invest in crypto.

Our initial focus is on crypto-native retail investors who want to stay exposed to the market without being consumed by it. DCA-ers, DeFi researchers and narrative traders active across multiple chains. These early adopters not only benefit from simplified on-chain actions, automated portfolios and AI-powered research, but also seed credibility and drive community growth. Building on this foundation, Crème will open the door for retail investors of all backgrounds to curate and buy diversified crypto portfolios in just a few clicks - making personal, structured, long-term investing simple and accessible.
