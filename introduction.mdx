# About Crème'ai

Crème'ai is an AI-powered DeFi app designed for all retail investors. With Crème'ai you can turn crypto narratives into portfolios within minutes, swap directly across chains and manage your portfolio all in one place. It is self-custodial, fully on-chain, gasless and yield-enabled.

# Why use Crème'ai

Crème’ai offers a simple, streamlined way to curate and manage your crypto portfolios. With Crème’ai you can:

-   Structure your portfolio clearly with weights using personal indexes, also referred to as [**Pies**](/using-creme#what-are-personal-indexes)

-   Turn crypto narratives into investable portfolios within minutes

-   Explore, remix, and share portfolio ideas with others

-   Manage and automate portfolio rebalancing across chains seamlessly

-   Access DeFi interactions such as lending and staking through a unified experience

By combining traditional index products with AI-powered workflows designed uniquely for crypto, Crème’ai offers a disciplined, personal, and accessible approach to crypto investing.

# Who is Crème'ai for?

Crème'ai is designed for anyone who wants a calmer and smarter way to invest in crypto.

### Newcomers

Crème'ai supports less experienced investors in learning how to structure crypto portfolios and approach risk more deliberately. Aside from this, on-chain complexities such as wallet setup or gas usage are all abstracted away to provide users with a smooth and authentic on-chain experience.

### Seasoned Web3 Natives

For more experienced users, Crème'ai offers a simple way to stay exposed to crypto markets without being constantly attached. Automate portfolio rebalancing across EVM chains, access on-chain actions and monitor positions more efficiently through a single, unified platform inspired by Web2 UX. A set it leave it approach, so that you can go touch more grass.
