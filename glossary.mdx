## Personal index

A user-defined grouping of assets around an idea or theme.

---

## Pie

The name used in Crème AI for a personal index.

---

## Narrative

A way of framing an idea or theme to organise thinking, not a prediction.

---

## Non-custodial

A design where users retain control of their assets at all times.

---

## On-chain

Actions that occur directly on the blockchain and affect wallet assets.

---

## Apply

To approve changes that update assets in a user’s wallet.
